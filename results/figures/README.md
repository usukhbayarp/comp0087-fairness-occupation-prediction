### `figures/`
Contains the publication-ready visualizations generated by `src/evaluation/plots.py`. 

* **`pareto_frontier.png`**: A scatter plot mapping all models on a 2D plane of Macro-F1 (Performance) vs. Average EO Gap (Fairness). Ideal for identifying the models that offer the best trade-off.
* **`job_bias_comparison.png`**: A horizontal bar chart highlighting the top 12 most biased occupations, comparing the Unmasked baseline against the Masked intervention.
* **`scaling_performance.png`** & **`scaling_fairness.png`**: Line charts demonstrating how performance and fairness metrics evolve as the Pythia model scales from 160M to 1.4B parameters.
* **`correlation_amplification_[Model].png`**: Regression plots replicating the original Bias-in-Bios analysis. Shows whether a specific model amplifies real-world gender imbalances by plotting the EO Gap against the percentage of females in the training data for each profession. 
* **`correlation_delta_[Model].png`**: Scatter plots showing the absolute fairness gain (Unmasked EO Gap minus Masked EO Gap) per profession, plotted against the real-world gender ratio.
